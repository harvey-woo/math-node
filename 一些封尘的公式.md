# 对数和幂函数

$$
  a^c = (\frac{1}{a})^{\frac{1}{c}}
$$

$$

  \frac{1}{x^a} = x^{-a}
$$


$$
  a^{b+c} = a^b \times a^c
$$

$$
  a^{b \times c} = (a^b)^c
$$



# 对数函数

$$
  \log_x{yz} = \log_x{y} + \log_x{z}
$$

$$
  \log_x{\frac{y}{z}} = \log_x{y} - \log_x{z}
$$

$$
  \log_x{y} = \frac{\log_a{y}}{\log_a{x}}
$$


$$
  \log_x{y} = \frac{\ln{y}}{\ln{x}} 
$$

$$
  \log_x{y^n} = n \times \log_x{y}
$$
