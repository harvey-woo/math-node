# 单位圆
![单位圆](./186px-Unit_circle.svg.png)
![单位圆](Snipaste_2019-05-06_21-33-54.png)

$$
  \sin{a} = \frac{PM}{OP}
$$

$$
  \cos{a} = \frac{OM}{OP}
$$

$$
  PM^2 + OM^2 = OP^2
$$

$$
  \sin{a}^2 + \cos{a}^2 = 1
$$
