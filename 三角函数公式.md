# 单位圆
![单位圆](./186px-Unit_circle.svg.png)
![单位圆](Snipaste_2019-05-06_21-33-54.png)

$$
  \sin{a} = \frac{PM}{OP}
$$

$$
  \cos{a} = \frac{OM}{OP}
$$

$$
  PM^2 + OM^2 = OP^2
$$

$$
  \sin^2{a} + \cos^2{a} = 1
$$

$$
  \tan{a} = \frac{PM}{OM} = \frac{\sin{a}}{\cos{a}}
  = \frac{TA}{OA} = TA
$$
$$
（a \neq k \pi + \frac{\pi}{2}）
$$
